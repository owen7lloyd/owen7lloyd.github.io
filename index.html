About

My name is Owen.

As a rising senior at Penn State, I've enjoyed working as a teaching assistant for Math 484 and being actively part of Alpha Kappa Psi, Schreyer Consulting Group, and Travel Club Tennis and Pickleball on campus.

During my junior year I began teaching junior tennis clinics and lessons at the Penn State Tennis Center, giving me the opportunity to work with kids of a wide variety of ages.

I have previously worked as a Data Science intern at Analog Devices on the OtoSenseAI team and as a Data Analytics intern at Croptix. I am currently working as a Production Analytics intern at Walt Disney Animation Studios.
