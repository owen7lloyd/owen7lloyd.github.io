About Me

My name is Owen.

I am an associate software engineer at MathWorks in the Engineering Development Group. While at MathWorks, I've had the opportunity to work as a customer-facing engineer, assisting customers with technical questions related to our control design and signal processing products. I've also designed customer demos using our Deep Learning and Financial products, along with developing and shipping updates to our internal Table and TreeTable widgets, used throughout MATLAB and Simulink.

Prior to MathWorks, I studied mathematics and data science at the Pennsylvania State University in the Schreyer Honors College. While at Penn State, I enjoyed writing a honors thesis in the mathematics department, working as a teaching assistant for Math 484 and being actively part of Alpha Kappa Psi and Travel Club Tennis and Pickleball on campus.

I have previously worked as a Production Analytics intern at Walt Disney Animation Studios, as a Data Science intern at Analog Devices on the OtoSenseAI team, and as a Data Analytics intern at Croptix.

Some of my interests include reading, film and TV, running, tennis, Philadelphia sports teams, listening to podcasts, and playing Catan.
